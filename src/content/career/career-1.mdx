##### Job Responsibilities

You'll be the primary point of contact for 15-20 mental health practices, guiding them through our comprehensive service offerings while ensuring exceptional results and client satisfaction.

<ul class="mb-10 flex list-inside list-disc flex-col gap-5 lg:mb-[60px]">
  <li>
    Manage client relationships from onboarding through ongoing success, serving as their strategic partner
  </li>
  <li>Coordinate service delivery across marketing, operations, and accreditation teams</li>
  <li>
    Conduct monthly strategy sessions with practice owners to review performance and adjust tactics
  </li>
  <li>Monitor client KPIs including patient acquisition, retention, and operational efficiency</li>
  <li>
    Identify upsell opportunities when practices are ready for additional services or higher tiers
  </li>
  <li>
    Ensure all client deliverables meet HIPAA compliance and ethical marketing standards
  </li>
  <li>
    Advocate for client needs internally and translate technical solutions into clear recommendations
  </li>
</ul>
