##### Job Responsibilities

You'll develop and execute social media strategies for 10-15 mental health practices, creating content that resonates with people seeking support while respecting the sensitivity of mental health topics.

<ul class="mb-10 flex list-inside list-disc flex-col gap-5 lg:mb-[60px]">
  <li>
    Create and schedule social media content across platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok)
  </li>
  <li>Develop content calendars aligned with mental health awareness dates and practice specialties</li>
  <li>
    Write compassionate, educational posts that reduce stigma and encourage help-seeking
  </li>
  <li>Monitor and respond to comments/messages while maintaining HIPAA compliance</li>
  <li>
    Analyze social media metrics and provide monthly performance reports to clients
  </li>
  <li>
    Collaborate with therapists to transform clinical insights into accessible social content
  </li>
  <li>
    Stay current on platform updates and mental health marketing best practices
  </li>
</ul>
